About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Associate with Cornell University. I received both my B.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2017 and 2021, respectively. I was a Research Fellow with National University of Singapore from 2021 to 2022.

Research Interests

My research broadly encompasses statistical signal processing and machine learning in cyber-physical networks, particularly in wireless communications, distributed learning, the Internet of things, and energy delivery systems. Currently, I am interested in:

  • Graph Signal Processing for network analysis

  • Information security and privacy in spatio-temporal data sharing

  • Multi-agent algorithms for distributed learning

  • Communication-efficient network AI
