About Me

I am an Assistant Professor with the State Key Laboratory of Internet of Things for Smart City (SKL-IOTSC) at the University of Macau. Previously, I received my B.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees from The Chinese University of Hong Kong. I worked as a Postdoctoral Associate with Cornell University.

We are recruiting motivated Ph.D. students (starting Fall 2026), Research Assistants, and visiting students interested in the intersection of statistical signal processing, machine learning, and wireless communications. Interested students are encouraged to contact me via email with their CV, transcript, and sample publications (if any).

Research Interests

My research broadly encompasses statistical signal processing and machine learning in wireless communications and cyber-physical networks. Currently, I am interested in:

  • Privacy aware learning

  • Edge intelligence

  • Graph signal processing

  • Generative AI for networking

  • Reinforcement learning for wireless communications
