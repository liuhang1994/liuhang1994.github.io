About Me

I am a Research Fellow with National University of Singapore (advisor: Prof. Rui Zhang) since September 2021. I received the B.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees (advisor: Prof. Angela Yingjun Zhang) from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2017 and 2021, respectively.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in statistical signal processing and optimization in wireless communications and networking, particularly in

  • Intelligent reflecting surface/Reconfigurable intelligent surface;

  • Wireless federated learning;

  • Multiuser MIMO/Massive MIMO.
