About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Associate with Cornell University. Previously, I received both my B.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees from The Chinese University of Hong Kong.

Research Interests

My research broadly encompasses statistical signal processing and machine learning in wireless communications and cyber-physical networks. Currently, I am interested in:

  • Security and privacy aware learning

  • Edge learning

  • Graph signal processing and learning over graphs

  • Reinforcement learning for network control and resource allocation
